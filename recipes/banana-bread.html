Banana Bread Recipe

My Favorite Whole 30 Banana Bread

This is my favorite Whole 30 compliant banana bread. There's not many ingredients and they're all natural. It's really easy to make and it really hits the spot when you're craving something sweet while doing Whole 30.

Here is a list of ingredients you will need to make this banana bread.

  • 2 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • 1 tsp alcohol free vanilla extract
  • 4 very ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts
