Banana Bread Recipe

My Favorite Whole 30 Banana Bread

This is my favorite Whole 30 compliant banana bread. There's not many ingredients and they're all natural. It's really easy to make and it really hits the spot when you're craving something sweet while doing Whole 30.

Here is a list of ingredients you will need to make this banana bread

  • 2 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • 1 tsp alcohol free vanilla extract
  • 4 very ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Steps to make this banana bread

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees Celsius (350 degrees Fahrenheit)
  2. In a mixing bowl combine the baking soda and almond flour
  3. Next add in the eggs, melted coconut oil, vanilla and 4 bananas, ensure all of the ingredients are mixed well
  4. Stir in the walnuts
  5. Line a baking tin with parchment paper (this will make your life a lot easier when trying to remove the bread after baking)
  6. Bake for 50-55 minutes
  7. IInsert a tooth pick or skewer into the bread and ensure it comes out clean before removing the bread from the oven. Let it cool for a few minutes before removing from the baking tin.
  8. Enjoy!
